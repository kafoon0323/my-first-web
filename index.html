Hi, we are CityU HCI Student Body

We are committed to integrating HCI, design, and technology to create a better campus experience through creativity and practice.

About the Student Union

CityU HCI Student Union is formed by students passionate about product, interaction design, and frontier technologies. It is both a learning community and a stage for project practice.

Learning & Exchange

We regularly host talks and workshops with professors and industry guests sharing HCI research and hands‑on experience.

Campus Projects

We initiate product and design projects for real campus needs, building portfolios and teamwork experience through practice.

Student Services

We collect student needs, provide guides, and bridge communication between professors and students.

Our Community

Join a vibrant community exploring HCI, design, and technology. We learn together and build real projects for campus life.
